SCALING: this is a Raft cluster. You may scale UP (raise `replicas`, then
`helm upgrade`; use an odd count like 3 or 5) but you must NEVER scale back
down - removed members stay in the Raft config and the cluster keeps trying to
reach them. There is intentionally no HorizontalPodAutoscaler.
